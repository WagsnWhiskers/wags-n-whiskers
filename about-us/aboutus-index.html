Our Mission

We are a small, all volunteer nonprofit rescue group dedicated to saving and re-homing and other small-breed dogs in need. Our mission is to evaluate, rehabilitate and place these dogs and cats in permanent loving homes. Incoming dogs and cats will be examined and treated by a veterinarian and cared for by experienced foster homes until they are adopted by an approved family. We will incorporate education and public outreach programs to help further these goals
