About Us

Wags 'n Whiskers is group dedicated to re-homing animals in need. Our mission is to match responible owners with potential pets to be place in their fur-ever loving homes. Through the help of Petfinder, Wags 'n Whiskers can search loacal shelters and fosters for adoptable pets; we also make it possible to send any email to these differnet shelters/foster to set up a meet up with a choosen animal. We will incorporate education information and public outreach programs to help further these goals.
